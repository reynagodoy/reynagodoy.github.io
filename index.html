Let's Talk

Human connection has never mattered more.

The difference between a mediocre event and an exceptional one? A clear purpose, a thoughtful flow, and someone holding the thread from start to finish.

That's what I do. I partner with organizations to design and produce experiences worth showing up for, holding the big idea while managing every detail that makes it real. Working together means you have one person who owns the whole thing: the venue, the vendors, the flow, the feeling, and the outcome it's meant to produce.

Because the best offsites aren't about transferring information anymore. They're about building the connective tissue, between people, between functions, and between a team and the work that actually matters.

What I design.

Company Offsites

Strategic and social gatherings that bring a team together with purpose, not just a change of scenery.

Leadership Retreats

Designed for leadership teams needing space to think, reconnect, and realign away from the day-to-day.

Community Experiences

For founder networks, impact accelerators, and communities where lasting connection is the outcome.

The Process.

01
Groundwork

We start with what matters: your goals, your people, and what this moment needs to accomplish. I ask the questions that surface what's already known — and what's still waiting to be asked.

02
Design, Planning and Production

I handle everything: venue sourcing, timelines, supplier coordination, budget tracking, event flow, and creative details. You stay informed, not overwhelmed.

03
Delivery on the Day

I own the day. Every detail is managed so that you and your team can be fully present and enjoy what you worked to create.

04
Reflect and Measure

Good events deserve a proper close. After the experience wraps, we run a structured retrospective, assess outcomes against the original goals, and document what worked, so every future gathering builds on a stronger one.

About Me

I am a contemplator of details and a gatherer of people, always have been.

My background spans production, program design, facilitation, space design, and creative direction. I think of myself equally as a creative and a doer, and this work sits exactly at that intersection. The through-line across all of it is the same: a belief that great experiences are never the product of one big gesture. They're the accumulation of many small, deliberate ones, and that accumulation is what I'm here to build.

Orchestrating is what I'm best at. I work across functions and perspectives with ease, synthesizing the goals of leadership teams, people ops, external partners, and internal committees into a single coherent experience. I think ten steps ahead and stay fully present. I plan for every scenario and leave room for the unexpected.

People tell me my events feel seamless. That's the intention. The seams are there, I just prefer you don't see them.
